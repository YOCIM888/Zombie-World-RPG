## 1. Faction Overview

The game has three main routes, with **Dawn Outpost** and **Doom Castle** being mutually exclusive:

| Route | Core NPCs | Ultimate Goal | Opposing Route |
|-------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| Dawn Outpost | Miss V, Su Xiaohan, Lilith, Mu Miaomiao, Leader | Dawn Captain Badge / Farmer God Ending / Love-Hate Ending | Doom Castle |
| Doom Castle | King, Queen | Crown Prince Identity Card / Love-Hate Ending | Dawn Outpost |
| Neutral | Dr. Chen, Luluwei, Liu Ruyan, Ma San, Old Wolf, Old Ma | Rocket Endings / New Force Ending / Mermaid Ending / Used as Gun Ending / On the Menu Ending / Elopement Ending | None |

**Key Rules:**
- After joining Dawn Outpost (obtaining Dawn Badge), you cannot take loans, work, or get a noble identity at Castle Bank
- After obtaining a Castle identity card, Outpost NPCs will refuse to interact with you
- However, **Castle Pass** will not trigger faction exclusion and will not disappear even if you hold the Dawn Captain Badge

## 2. Dawn Outpost Route

**Location:** Dawn Outpost → Talk to Miss V

Miss V is the strongest combat instructor at the Outpost. Her quest line is centered around "trading combat supplies for weapons", with rewards progressing from low to high tier.

| V2 Hemostasis Training | 30 | Tourniquet ×5 | Knuckle Knife |
| V3 Serum Quest | 60 | Anti-Infection Serum ×8 | Dragon Stealing Sword |
| V4 Hard Trial | 90 | Field Medical Kit ×8 | P226 + 9×19mm ×50 |
| V5 Emergency Supplies | 120 | Medical First Aid Kit ×6 + Anti-Infection Serum ×6 | UZI + 9×19mm ×50 |
| V6 Ultimate Trial | 150 | All 15 medicines ×2 each | M4A1 + 5.56×45mm NATO ×50 |

**List of Medicines Required for V6 (2 of each):**
Band-Aid, Tourniquet, Antibiotic, Anti-Infection Serum, Surgery Kit, Universal Injection, Medical First Aid Kit, Field Medical Kit

**Affection Increase Methods:** Daily chat +1, gift giving +1, complete quests

### 2.4 Mu Miaomiao's Quest Line (⭐ Core Story)

**Location:** Dawn Outpost → Talk to Mu Miaomiao

This is the **most touching quest line** in the entire game and the only path to Ending 9: "I'm Not the Farmer God".

#### Prerequisite Chain

```
Mu Miaomiao Affection 150 → Unlock secrets → Get Farming Master Badge
                                                        ↓
                                    Go to Watchtower → Wreath Tombstone → Inspect (requires badge)
                                                        ↓
                                    Dig (requires Shovel equipped)
                                                        ↓
                                    Get Miaomiao's Diary
                                                        ↓
                                    Return to Mu Miaomiao → Unlock "Buried Past"
```

#### Step 1: Obtain Farming Master Badge

**Condition:** Mu Miaomiao Affection ≥ 150

Affection Increase Methods:
- Buy seeds (1 cigarette → 3 seeds, small affection increase)
- Take care of experimental field (once daily, 2 turns, affection +1~3, get 1 random seed)
- Daily chat +1, gift giving +1

**Reward:** Farming Master Badge

#### Step 2: Obtain Miaomiao's Diary

**Location:** Mountain Top Abandoned Watchtower → Select "Wreath Tombstone"

1. Select "Inspect": Requires Farming Master Badge
2. Select "Dig": Requires Shovel equipped → Get **Miaomiao's Diary**

#### Step 3: Quest Chain "Buried Past"

| Quest | Requirement | Reward |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| 1 Let's Go Picnic Together | Carrot ×3 + Potato ×3 | Affection +20 |
| 2 Cross-Time Thoughts | High-Proof Liquor ×5 + Rye ×10 | Affection +30 |
| 3 Expand Experimental Field | 15 different crops ×1 each | Affection +50, **Prairie Bow** + Arrows ×30 |

**Prairie Bow** (52 damage / 52% crit / 520 integrity / arrows / legendary) — Story-exclusive weapon.

#### Ending Trigger

After completing Quest 3, **immediately trigger** Ending 9: "I'm Not the Farmer God".

## 3. Doom Castle Route

### 3.1 King's Quest Line

**Location:** Doom Castle → Talk to King

**Prerequisite:** Must first buy Noble Identity Card (25 cigarettes)

| Quest | Requirement | Reward | Rank |
|-------|-------------|--------|------|
| 1 Queen's Trouble | Glucose Solution ×10 | Viscount Identity Card | Rank 2 |
| 2 King's Banquet | High-Proof Liquor ×10 | Earl Identity Card | Rank 3 |
| 3 King's Hobby | Cigarettes ×10 + Hundred Flavor Beer ×5 + Power Beer ×5 | Marquis Identity Card | Rank 4 |
| 4 Immortality | Universal Injection ×3 | Duke Identity Card | Rank 5 |
| 5 Eliminate Dissidents | Assassinate Outpost Leader | Crown Prince Identity Card | Rank 6 |

**⚠️ Critical Choice:** Quest 5 requires assassinating the Outpost Leader. The Leader's combat power equals that of the Zombie King (400 HP, 110 damage, 25% dodge). After successful assassination:
- Leader dies, Outpost functions limited
- Can no longer interact with Miss V, Su Xiaohan, or Lilith
- Cannot obtain Dawn Captain Badge
- Triggers Ending 3: "Addiction to Power"

**Underground Key:** After reaching Crown Prince (Rank 6), you can give the King an AWM Sniper Rifle to exchange for the Underground Key. The underground area has a small nuclear generator, which is a key item for Dr. Chen's rocket chain.

## 5. Neutral Route

### 5.1 Companion System

#### Liu Ruyan (Semi-Infected Woman)

**Location:** City Center Comprehensive Mall

**Discovery Text:** "You find a semi-infected woman lying in the corner, she still has a trace of consciousness."

**Rescue Condition:** Inject 3 Anti-Infection Serums within 30 days

**After Rescue Effect:** Automatically collects supplies daily (food/drink/fruit/ammo)

**⚠️ Overdue Failure:** Woman completely turns into zombie, irreversible.

**💡 Tip:** After discovering Liu Ruyan, Survival Notes will automatically add a rescue countdown reminder.

#### Luluwei (Nurse Zombie)

**Location:** Suburb Large Hospital

**Recruitment Condition:** Feed canned food to raise affection to 150 (non-canned food gives no affection), then select "Take Home"

**After Recruitment Effect:** Automatically brings 1 medicine daily

### 5.2 Dr. Chen's Rocket Chain

**Location:** Biochemical Research Institute → Talk to Dr. Chen

This is the only path to the 3 space endings and the most complex quest chain in the game.

#### Prerequisite Chain

```
Dr. Chen Trade (30 Serums → M700) → Defeat Giant Puppet → Rocket Quest 1 (Small Nuclear Generator)
                                                                                ↓
                                                            Requires Castle Crown Prince Identity → Give King AWM → Underground Key
                                                                                ↓
                                                            Underground Area Get Generator
                                                                                ↓
                                                            Rocket Quest 2 (Pure Energy ×15, 1 per day)
                                                                                ↓
                                                            Rocket Quest 3 (Medicine ×10 + Food ×10 + Drink ×10)
                                                                                ↓
                                                            Rocket Repaired → 3 Choices
```

#### Doctor Initial Trade

**Requirement:** Anti-Infection Serum ×30

**Reward:** M700 + 7.62×51mm ×30

#### Rocket Quest 1: Small Nuclear Generator

**Prerequisite:** Doctor trade completed + Defeat Giant Zombie Puppet (Space Base)

**Obtainment Method:** Castle Crown Prince (Rank 6) → Give King AWM → Get Underground Key → Underground Area obtain

#### Rocket Quest 2: Pure Energy ×15

**Source:** Research Institute Energy Mining Well, can extract 1 per day (requires completing Quest 1 to activate)

#### Rocket Quest 3: Survival Supplies

**Requirement:** Medicine ×10 + Food ×10 + Drink ×10

#### Launch Choices

After rocket is repaired, go to Space Base Launch Center, 3 choices:

| Choice | Condition | Ending |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| Board rocket alone | None | Ending 6: Fly to Space (Game Over) |
| Take important people with you | V/Xiaohan/Lilith affection all ≥150 | Ending 7: Spark of Hope (Game Over) |
| I won't go, Doctor go | None | Ending 8: Stay Here (Can continue) |

**Ending 8 Hidden Effect:** Doctor leaves Improved Antibody Needle manufacturing equipment, can claim 1 daily (reduces infection by 90%).

### 5.3 Old Wolf/Mermaid Legend (⭐ Core Story)

**Location:** Old Residential Area → Survivor Old Wolf

This is the only path to Ending 13: "Mermaid Legend" and one of the longest quest chains in the game.

#### Prerequisite Chain

```
Electric Fish Tank (City Center Comprehensive Mall)
  │
  ├→ Put epic fish → Get Lightning Scale
  └→ Put legendary fish → Get Thunder Scale
        │
        ↓
Old Wolf Quest 1: Scale Collection (5 Lightning Scales)
        │
        ↓
Old Wolf Quest 2: Collector (3 Thunder Scales)
        │
        ↓
Unlock Property Warehouse → Injured Mermaid
        │
        ↓
Submit scales to raise Mermaid affection to 150 → Get Sea Song Trident
        │
        ↓
Old Wolf Quest 3: Preparation Before Going to Sea (5 Gasoline +5 Food +5 Drink)
        │  Reward: Desert Eagle + .50 AE ×30
        ↓
Old Wolf Quest 4: Giant Sea Corpse (Boss fight, ranged only)
        │
        ↓
Ending 13: Mermaid Legend
```

#### Electric Fish Tank (Scale Obtainment)

**Location:** City Center Comprehensive Mall → Electric Fish Tank

After catching fish on the island, come here to put them in:
- **Epic fish** → Get **Lightning Scale** (epic quality)
- **Legendary fish** → Get **Thunder Scale** (legendary quality)
- Common fish → Get electrocuted, no reward

⚠ Touching tank directly has 50% chance to take electric shock damage (-50 HP).

#### Old Wolf Basic Functions

| Function | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Chat | Random dialogue |
| Barter | 3 any food → 1 random common-rare medicine (once daily) |

#### Old Wolf Quest Chain

##### Quest 1: Scale Collection

**Requirement:** Lightning Scale ×5

**Tip:** Game will提示 you to go to mall electric fish tank

**Reward:** Old Wolf Affection +10, unlock Quest 2

##### Quest 2: Collector

**Requirement:** Thunder Scale ×3

**Reward:** Old Wolf Affection +15, unlock **Property Warehouse** (can access injured Mermaid)

##### Mermaid Affection System

In Property Warehouse, submit scales to injured Mermaid:
- Lightning Scale → Affection +2
- Thunder Scale → Affection +5

When affection reaches 150, Mermaid fully recovers, gives **Sea Song Trident** (melee weapon / legendary quality), and unlocks Old Wolf Quest 3.

##### Quest 3: Preparation Before Going to Sea

**Requirement:** Gasoline ×5 + Any food ×5 + Any drink ×5

**Reward:** Old Wolf Affection +20, **Desert Eagle** + .50 AE ×30

##### Quest 4: Giant Sea Corpse

**Condition:** Must equip ranged weapon and have ammo (melee invalid!)

**Boss Stats:** Giant Sea Corpse — 600 HP, 80 damage, 8% dodge

**Reward:** After defeating, trigger **Ending 13: Mermaid Legend** (Game Over)

### 5.4 Old Ma's Quest Chain (⭐ Core Story)

**Location:** Abandoned Factory Warehouse → Old Ma (Warehouse Guardian)

This is the only path to Ending 14: "Used as a Gun" and the darkest quest chain.

#### Prerequisite

To trigger Old Ma's quest chain, you must first use the "Doom Contraband" option at **Chain Large Warehouse Supermarket**.

#### Quest Chain

Old Ma has 7 quest steps, each requiring completing the previous to progress:

| Step | Quest | Requirement | Reward |
|------|-------|-------------|--------|
| 1 | Find Files | Collect **Crime Data ×10** from Abandoned Police Station File Cabinet | Cigarettes ×3 |

| 6 | Eliminate Dr. Chen | Eliminate **Dr. Chen** at Biochemical Research Institute | Cigarettes ×25 |
| 7 | Underground Subway Tunnel | Go to Underground Subway Tunnel - Wait for Old Ma | Trigger ending |

**⚠ Key Tips:**
- Quest 1 Crime Data obtained at **Abandoned Police Station → Search File Cabinet** (30% chance)
- Quest 4 Assassinate King requires selecting "Assassinate King" option inside Castle
- Quest 4 cannot hold Castle identity card, otherwise Old Ma refuses
- Quest 5 requires selecting "Eliminate Old Zhao" during interaction at Military Checkpoint
- Quest 6 requires selecting "Eliminate Doctor" during interaction at Biochemical Research Institute

#### Boss Stats Reference

#### Ending Trigger

## 10. Recommended Route Planning

### Route A: Full Story Experience (Recommended for First Play)

This route allows you to experience the most story content in one playthrough and achieve Ending 9 and Ending 10.

```
Phase 1 (Early Game): Outpost Basic Setup
├─ Join Dawn Outpost
├─ Progress Miss V's line to V4 (90 affection)
├─ Progress Su Xiaohan's line to XH3 (90 affection)
├─ Raise Lilith's affection (use recycle/repair services)
├─ Raise Mu Miaomiao's affection (buy seeds + take care of field)
└─ Rescue Liu Ruyan (mall, 3 serums within 30 days)

Phase 2 (Mid Game): Farming Line + Companions
├─ Mu Miaomiao 150 affection → Farming Master Badge
├─ Watchtower tombstone dig → Miaomiao's Diary
├─ Mu Miaomiao Quests 1-3 → Prairie Bow → Ending 9
├─ Rescue Luluwei (hospital, feed cans)
├─ Raise Miss V/Xiaohan/Lilith to 150 affection
└─ Raise Leader's affection

Phase 3 (Late Game): Voice Recorder Chain + Queen Line
├─ All NPCs 150 affection → Dawn Captain Badge
├─ Riverside Campsite → Smelly Tent → Strange Voice Recorder
├─ Leader's Quests 1-3 → Love Token
├─ Queen's Quests 1-4 → Castle Pass → Ending 10
└─ Already achieved 2 endings, can continue playing

Phase 4 (End Game): Rocket Line (Optional)
├─ Doctor Trade (30 serums)
├─ Defeat Giant Puppet
├─ ⚠ This step requires Castle identity, mutually exclusive with Captain Badge
└─ If you want to experience rocket ending, need to choose Castle route in new playthrough
```
